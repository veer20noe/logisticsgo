About Us

Logistics Go is a trusted and professional vehicle transport company based in Gurugram, Haryana. We specialize in safe, secure, and timely car and bike transportation services across India.

With years of hands-on logistics experience and a dedicated operations team, we focus primarily on damage-free vehicle relocation. Whether you need to transport your car to another state or shift your bike to a different city, our structured handling process ensures complete safety at every step.
